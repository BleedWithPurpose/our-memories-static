12A VS 12C volley match
2 November 2024

During the wait in the long line for the graduation photo taking, the 12A and C class boys challenged each other to a volley tournament. And thus all the boys from both sides were in agreement to meet at 6 15 on Saturday morning
Although some were late, and some couldn't wake up, the majority of both classes graduates were present. And the volley match commenced
With unaish and the 12A boys on one side to balance out the number of players, a beat of 5 sets game commenced with the 12C group winning 3-1. Albeit, most of the 12A boys didn't played volley beforehand, thus we commend their openness to trying new stuff. We are looking forward to more volley battles, and so are they.

Secret Santa
29 March 2024

The whole of 12C class wanted to do a gift exchange thing, so we did. We all got names like a month beforehand, packed our gifts and headed near the airport. Special guest Ma'aal was here all the way from Malé, the date was very difficult to set because there were so many other things happening aswell.

After waiting for everyone to arrive, we moved to a little grassy location near the aiport, and all sat down. We decided to open each gift seperatly, and guess who might have gotten us the gift, even though mostly we guessed wrong.

It was veru fun and endearing, alot of cool gifts and fun moments, and ofcourse we barely made it home before roadha veehlun time.

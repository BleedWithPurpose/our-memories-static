Class BBQ 1
4 November 2023

On November 4, 2023, the Nukiyavaakudhin ventured outdoors to a spacious clearing near our beloved Yaaseen's house to make "memories"—said memories mainly consisting of food. A barbecue, to be exact.

Starting off strong, our cooking speed was astronomical. To speed up the process of making the chicken edible, a few girls went over to Yaaseen's house, where they had to navigate and make use of incredibly high-tech machines (a stove) which they unfortunately didn't know how to use. However, with the aid of Yaaseen's father, they were able to cook the chicken and return to base. Some also went to Manaal's house to spot the infamous Bear cat.

However, this was not our only setback. We were missing a crucial member of the gang: Bodey. Now, Bodey was in deep slumber, seemingly unaware of the dazzling feast that awaited him. So the boys took it upon themselves to go fetch him, so that he wouldn't miss out on all the tasty goodness.

With garlic bread made from Zeeshaan's flavorful garlic butter, roasted potatoes, sausages, and chicken, we were ready for the feast after so long. All the little Nukiyavaakudhins attacked the food like famished little critters and enjoyed our time together very well.

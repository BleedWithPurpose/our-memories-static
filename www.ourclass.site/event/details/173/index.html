Last day at class
29 August 2024

Last day of regular school, Mocks will start next week and after that we won't get the class.
The business students sold food on the last day, giving us the opportunity to stay at school the whole day without going to interval. There was also a lucky draw from those who bought food.
In the period before break, we cleaned the class and distributed "vaarutha mudhaa". Molhey sirs gudhan still remained tho.
For maths period the classes changed to the way it was before, and we got to spend some time with the OG 12C. Molhey sir also planned to treat us with food that night, very out of the blue.
We also got to learn about some Inayath miss lore
